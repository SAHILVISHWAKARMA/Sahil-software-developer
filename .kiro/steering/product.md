# Product

A personal developer portfolio for Sahil Vishwakarma, a Full Stack Software Developer with 3.5+ years of experience. The site is a single-page application showcasing skills, work experience, projects, achievements, and a contact form.

Deployed as a static site to Firebase Hosting at `https://sahilvishwakarma.dev`.

## Sections
- Hero, About, Skills, Experience, Projects, Achievements, Contact
- Fixed navbar with smooth scroll navigation
- Dark/light theme toggle (defaults to dark)
- Resume download button
- Contact form powered by EmailJS
